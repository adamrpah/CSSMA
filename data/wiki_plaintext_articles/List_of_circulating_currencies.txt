List of circulating currencies

2007 Schools Wikipedia Selection. Related subjects: Currency

   This list of circulating currencies contains the 194 current official
   or de facto currencies of the 192 United Nations member states, one UN
   observer state, three partially recognized sovereign states, six
   unrecognized countries, and 33 dependencies. All dependencies on the
   table below will be italicized. Partially or wholly unrecognized
   countries are in bold. All dependencies listed here use a currency
   other than that of the sovereign state that they belong to.
